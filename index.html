About

My start with photography was in 2004, when I started taking photos just for fun. I enjoyed shooting landscapes and things like that, never intending that this hobby of mine would ever become anything more. Taking photos gave me a good creative outlet and I had fun with it. As it turned out, about 4 years later, I started to view my "real" job as a little, shall we say, unfulfilling. So I did a little soul searching and committed to pursue photography on a professional level. I was excited and scared at the same time: after all, shooting a wedding is major responsibility -- no second chances, right? I took the time to train and educate myself, and worked as an assistant for awhile at weddings and after many weddings as a second, I decided it was time to step out on my own and I've never looked back. I am absolutely thrilled to work with brides and grooms and to help them capture their most precious moments together! Check out my work here, on social media, and send me a message! I look forward to hearing form you!

-Jeff
